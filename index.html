Welcome to the FESH Project

Field Evaluation of Soil Health (FESH) is a 3-year research project conducted in New Brunswick, Canada. The goal is to collect, review, and analyze soil health data to support sustainable agriculture.

Project Objectives

  • 📍 Collect in-field soil data from farmers and field staff
  • 🔬 Evaluate physical, chemical, and biological indicators of soil health
  • ✅ Review and approve data by experts
  • 📊 Provide clean, validated datasets for researchers and decision-makers
  • 🌾 Support sustainable agriculture in New Brunswick

Project Duration

2024 – 2027

Partners

Department of Agriculture, Research Institutions, Local Farmers
